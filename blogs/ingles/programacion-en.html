That's how i started

In one of my early jobs, someone showed me the keyboard combination "Shift + =" in Excel, and from there, all the math I learned in school became useful.

I started showcasing the results of my work using Excel, such as inventory management, performance reports, sales projections, and more, all created with templates I designed myself.

I didn't stop there as I loved the utility of this tool. Recognizing its parallelism with other tools, I continued studying and learning different subjects, delving into topics like VBA (Visual Basic for Applications) and .NET, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, design systems, and user utility. It opened up a world of possibilities for creating digital solutions.

I noticed the difficulty many people have with Excel, which I also experienced when I was overwhelmed by its numerous possibilities. I understood the importance of creating systems that are user-friendly and provide solutions rather than complex problems. That's why I expanded my studies to UX/UI design, making it a philosophy in my design process.

Conta Gourmet

Personal project, which results in an Excel template, programmed with VBA, to manage the billing and administration process in restaurants with table service and homes

The solution was born from the desire to solve problems that he lived at@elgourmetdelamami as head chef, to manage administrative processes.

The template focuses on helping to easily manage the actual costing of the products, the standardization of the recipes, the management of purchases, sales, inventories and supply of raw materials, as well as the management of clients for product delivery.

As a positive result, customer service processes were streamlined, in data collection and orders, for their correct production and effective distribution of the products at the table and at home.

If you are interested in the template Conta Gourmet for restaurant management, don't hesitate to ask 😁.
