My Philosophy on Movement

In my youth, skateboarding energized me to move my body, avoiding a sedentary lifestyle, but I felt the need for teamwork.

When I started my professional life, my skateboarding friends discovered Ultimate Frisbee, and I joined them in this completely new sport at the time. I became fully hooked, and from there, my passion for movement was born.

As a group, we helped grow this sport in the Cali region and surrounding municipalities. Through self-teaching and dedication, we improved as athletes. When we started, we were no more than 14 players; today, there are over 500 players in the area.

In my quest to improve my personal training, I discovered Yoga, which showed me that with small, conscious steps, significant progress can be made physically, mentally, and emotionally.

From then on, I understood that sports and movement are not just hobbies but an essential part of being—helping in personal and professional life by maintaining balance and self-awareness.

Projects

These are some of the projects I have participated in or where I provide my services as a consultant and designer.

Saludo al Sol

Ultimate Frisbee tournament held during the holiday weekend in August, which in 2023 will celebrate its 17th edition.

I have been a collaborator in this project for 14 years, working as the logistics leader for the game system, responsible for collecting data from each match. For this task, I mainly use Excel and VBA.

The main responsibility of this role is to provide the final rankings, including the Champion and Runner-up in each category, as well as individual player statistics (Goals, Assists, Blocks).

Working in this tournament has motivated me every year to improve my skills in design and web development. I have taken it as a personal project that I have been prototyping using No-code tools, and I named it:

Let's Play Ultimate

If you are interested in managing game systems or Ultimate Frisbee tournaments, or if you want to know more about my project prototype, feel free to contact me 👇.

Acro Básico

A personal project that resulted in an Excel template programmed with VBA to manage invoicing and administration for restaurants offering table service and deliveries.

This solution was created to solve problems I experienced at @elgourmetdelamami as the head chef, helping to manage administrative processes.

The template focuses on simplifying real product costing, recipe standardization, purchasing, sales, inventory management, raw material provisioning, and customer management for product delivery.

As a positive outcome, customer service processes were streamlined, improving order-taking accuracy and ensuring efficient production and delivery of products for both table service and takeout.

If you're interested in the Conta Gourmet template for restaurant administration, feel free to ask! 😁
